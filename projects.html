A visual reference for cranioskeletal anatomy and postnatal development in C57BL/6J mice
A collection of labeled reference images and drawings generated from microCT scans and immunohistochemical labeling of embryonic and postnatal C57BL/6J mice.
Includes images of:
  • The first four weeks of mandibular, vertebral, and cranial base, development,
  • Arterial morphology
  • Sections through the bonny inner ear, and
  • Images showing conservation between humans and mice.

An appendix from my Ph.D. thesis in the Cox lab at Seattle Children's Research Institute.
